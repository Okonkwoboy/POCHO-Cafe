About Us

Pocho is a unique dining experience by okonkwo family that combines the rich flavors and cultural influences of Africa with a contemporary twist. Our menu features a variety of dishes inspired by the diverse cuisines of the African continent, including traditional staples such as stewed meats, grilled fish, and flavorful spices, as well as innovative fusion dishes that incorporate international ingredients and cooking techniques. Our aim is to provide our customers with a warm, inviting atmosphere where they can enjoy delicious food, drink, and live music, all while immersing themselves in the vibrant cultures of Africa.
